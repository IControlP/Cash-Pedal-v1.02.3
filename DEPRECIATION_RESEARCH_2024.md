## Mileage Impact Adjustments

### Annual Mileage Multipliers
- **Under 5,000 miles/year:** 0.85 (15% better retention)
- **5,000-8,000 miles/year:** 0.92 (8% better retention)
- **8,000-10,000 miles/year:** 0.96 (4% better retention)
- **10,000-12,000 miles/year:** 1.00 (baseline)
- **12,000-15,000 miles/year:** 1.08 (8% worse retention)
- **15,000-18,000 miles/year:** 1.15 (15% worse retention)
- **18,000-22,000 miles/year:** 1.25 (25% worse retention)
- **Over 22,000 miles/year:** 1.35 (35% worse retention, capped)

### High-Mileage Threshold Exceptions
Some vehicles handle high mileage better:
- **Trucks (Toyota, Ford, Chevy):** -20% mileage penalty
- **Honda/Toyota cars:** -15% mileage penalty
- **Subarus:** -10% mileage penalty

## Market Trends (2024-2025)

### Currently Appreciating Trends
1. **Body-on-frame SUVs** (4Runner, Land Cruiser, Bronco)
2. **Off-road capable vehicles** (Wrangler, Gladiator, Bronco)
3. **Manual transmission sports cars** (becoming rare)
4. **Certain collector vehicles** (Corvette, 911, Supra)

### Rapid Depreciation Trends
1. **Entry-level EVs** (technology obsolescence)
2. **Luxury sedans** (SUV preference shift)
3. **Small cars** (market preference for SUVs/trucks)
4. **COVID-era overprice vehicles** (normalizing values)
